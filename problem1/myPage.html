My Favorites Movie

The Matrix and 2012 are two of my favorite movies. I enjoy feature films that combine the action and post-apocalyptic genres together.

The Matrix

It depicts a dystopian future in which humanity is unknowingly trapped inside a simulated reality, the Matrix, which intelligent machines have created to distract humans while using their bodies as an energy source.

2012

2012 is a 2009 American science-fiction, apocalyptic, disaster film directed and co-written by Roland Emmerich. The film is about a global cataclysmic event that is bringing an end to the world in the year 2012 and tells the heroic struggle of the survivors.
